Enum FieldChainRequirements

Namespace
JsonApiDotNetCore.Queries.Internal.Parsing
Assembly
JsonApiDotNetCore.dll

Used internally when parsing subexpressions in the query string parsers to indicate requirements when resolving a chain of fields. Note these may be interpreted differently or even discarded completely by the various parser implementations, as they tend to better understand the characteristics of the entire expression being parsed.

[Flags]
public enum FieldChainRequirements

Fields

Indicates a single AttrAttribute, optionally preceded by a chain of RelationshipAttributes.

EndsInToMany = 4

Indicates a single HasManyAttribute, optionally preceded by a chain of RelationshipAttributes.

EndsInToOne = 2

Indicates a single HasOneAttribute, optionally preceded by a chain of RelationshipAttributes.

IsRelationship = EndsInToOne | EndsInToMany

Indicates one or a chain of RelationshipAttributes.
